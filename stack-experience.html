The Experience Stack

Purpose-built wellness tools optimized for speed to market, luxury brand aesthetics, and a compelling pre-opening member community. Designed for a social-forward bathhouse that needs to capture early community interest now, activate founding memberships before opening, and integrate smoothly with operations at launch. The tradeoff: billing and community live in third-party platforms, making future migrations more effort-intensive.

DNS & infrastructure
Cloudflare
DNS Primary pick

Best DNS management available — free CDN, DDoS protection, and DNSSEC. Point all domain registrars to Cloudflare nameservers. Manage SSL, redirects, and email authentication records from one place.

Google Workspace
Email

Business Starter. Set up SPF then DKIM first — DMARC requires one of these in place. Create key aliases immediately: hello@, members@, bookings@, press@. Email is a brand asset from day one.

Website & waitlist
Framer
Landing page Primary pick

Most design-forward no-code builder available. React-based output, fast to publish, built-in CMS. The natural choice for a brand-first luxury soft launch. Pricing updated Oct 2025 — verify current plan before purchasing.

Beehiiv
Waitlist + email Primary pick

Built for audience-building. Free plan covers up to 2,500 subscribers with unlimited sends and referral mechanics. Paid Scale plan unlocks A/B testing, advanced analytics, and ads. Connect Framer → Beehiiv → HubSpot via Zapier.

CRM & data
HubSpot CRM
CRM Primary pick

The central spine. Free CRM handles up to 1M contacts. Starter plan removes HubSpot branding and unlocks basic automation. Set up contact properties from day one — source, founding_member flag, and tool IDs for Phase 2 and 3 connections.

Google Shared Drives
Cloud storage

Shared Drives (not personal folders) ensure the business retains file ownership as the team grows. Structure: /Brand /Operations /Legal /Finance /Marketing /Programming /Membership /Vendors /Buildout.

GA4 + Search Console
Analytics

Install GA4 via Framer's custom code injection from day one. Track signups, scroll depth, and traffic source. Connect Search Console immediately — SEO data accumulates from the moment the domain resolves.

Zapier
Integration

Core zap: Beehiiv signup → HubSpot contact with source tracking. Expand as the stack grows. Graduate to direct API webhooks when volume and complexity justify it.

Membership management
Glofox
Membership Primary pick

Modern, mobile-first membership management with a branded member app. Strong tier logic, class scheduling, and recurring billing. Integrates with Stripe and Mailchimp. Purpose-built for boutique wellness businesses.

⚠ No public pricing — demo required. Multiple independent user reviews flag hidden fees, unexpected price increases, and pressure toward multi-year contracts. Request all terms and full pricing in writing before signing. Confirm month-to-month availability and data export capability.

Stripe + Memberstack
Membership (alternative)

If Glofox contract terms are unsatisfactory, Stripe Billing combined with Memberstack gives complete billing control. More developer configuration upfront — but billing history is fully portable and not tied to any wellness-specific platform.

Community & events
Circle.so
Community platform Primary pick

Branded community platform — private discussion spaces, member profiles, event announcements, and live streams. Creates a destination founding members can actually inhabit before the physical space opens. Note: Circle charges a transaction fee on payments processed through the platform.

Luma
Event ticketing Primary pick

Social-first event platform built for cultural communities. Beautiful event pages, Stripe-integrated ticketing, QR check-ins, and a discovery network that helps Miami's creative community find SOUNDBATH events. Free plan available; paid plan removes the platform fee. Verified lower blended fees than Eventbrite at comparable volume.

Beehiiv referrals
Referral system

Beehiiv's Scale plan includes built-in referral mechanics. Use this before purchasing a dedicated referral tool. Waitlist referrals and founding member invite codes both run natively through Beehiiv with HubSpot attribution.

Referral-to-booking pipeline

The original brief specifically calls out a "referral / guest booking system" — meaning a flow where a referred person can not only join the waitlist but book a visit or founding membership directly. Beehiiv handles the referral mechanics (tracking who referred whom), but the booking endpoint needs to be intentional. Here's how it works in this stack:

A member shares a referral link (generated in Beehiiv or as a Luma guest code). The referred person lands on a Luma event or Glofox membership page and books directly. Source tracking in HubSpot confirms the referral attribution. This covers social referrals and event-based booking in one clean flow.

Glofox referral booking

Glofox supports referral codes natively — a member can share a discount or access code that gives the referred person a path to book a founding membership or trial visit. Confirm this feature is included in the plan before signing. Map the Glofox referral ID back to HubSpot contact source field for clean attribution data.

Booking & operations
Book4Time
Booking + operations Primary pick

Established industry standard for luxury spa operations — used by Four Seasons Spa, Mandarin Oriental Spa, and other tier-one wellness brands. Handles guest booking, therapist scheduling, POS, consent forms, and guest profiles. Strong North American support presence and a mature, well-documented API.

Zenoti
Booking (alternative)

More modern API and deeper standalone wellness business logic. Used by Waldorf Astoria Spa and luxury standalone wellness chains. Better native membership management than Book4Time. The Architect Stack uses Zenoti as its primary ops platform — if that architecture feels like the right long-term fit, start Zenoti conversations now alongside Book4Time.

RFID, POS, onboarding & analytics
Gantner
RFID / lockers

European spa and wellness RFID specialist. Wristband-based locker access, cashless spending throughout the facility. Used across major European bathhouses and US spa properties. Get quotes from both Gantner and Ojmar — evaluate after site measurements are confirmed.

Toast
POS

Best if F&B is a major revenue driver. Strong kitchen display and tip flows. If retail is primary, evaluate Book4Time's native POS first — reducing system count reduces integration complexity and ongoing maintenance.

Smartwaiver
Waivers

Digital health intake forms, consent docs, and waiver management. Auto-syncs to guest profiles. Embed in the pre-arrival email flow — don't make waivers a front-desk bottleneck on opening day.

Persona
ID verification

Best-in-class ID and selfie match, age verification. Integrate into the pre-arrival flow, not at the front desk. Pay-per-verification keeps cost proportional to actual usage.

Registrar strategy

Consolidate at Namecheap or Cloudflare Registrar (at-cost pricing, no markup). Point all nameservers to Cloudflare DNS for centralized redirects and SSL management.

Email authentication order

SPF first, then DKIM — DMARC cannot be configured without at least one in place. All three records live in Cloudflare DNS. Never send outbound email before all three are active.

Core principle: HubSpot is the relationship spine. Every tool that touches a customer syncs back here. Glofox holds billing during Phase 2 — establish the tool ID field in HubSpot contacts from day one so a future Stripe migration has a clean target if needed.
