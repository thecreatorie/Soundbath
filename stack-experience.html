DNS & infrastructure
CloudflareFree
DNS Recommended

Best DNS management available — free CDN, DDoS protection, DNSSEC. Point all domain registrars to Cloudflare nameservers. Manage SSL, redirects, and email authentication records from one dashboard.

Google Workspace$12/user/mo
Email

Business Starter. Set up SPF then DKIM first — DMARC requires one of these in place. Key aliases to create immediately: hello@, members@, bookings@, press@. Email is a brand asset from day one.

Website & waitlist
Framer$30/mo (Pro)
Landing page Recommended

Most design-forward no-code builder available. React-based output, fast to publish, built-in CMS. Ideal for a brand-first luxury soft launch. Pricing updated Oct 2025 — Pro plan is now $30/mo. Basic at $10/mo is too limited for a client site.

BeehiivFree → $49/mo Scale
Waitlist + email Recommended

Built for audience-building. Free plan covers up to 2,500 subscribers with unlimited sends, referral mechanics, and a subscribe widget. Scale plan at $49/mo (annually) unlocks A/B testing and advanced analytics. Connect Framer → Beehiiv → HubSpot via Zapier.

CRM & data
HubSpot CRMFree → $15/seat/mo
CRM Recommended

The central spine. Free CRM handles up to 1M contacts. Starter at $15/seat/mo removes HubSpot branding and unlocks basic automation. Set up contact properties now: source, founding_member, glofox_member_id from day one.

Google Shared DrivesIncluded
Cloud storage

GA4 + Search ConsoleFree

Zapier~$20/mo

Industry standard for luxury hotel spas — Four Seasons, Mandarin Oriental, and other tier-one properties. Handles guest booking, therapist scheduling, POS, consent forms, and guest profiles. Strong North American support. Best if hotel partnership at Phase 3 is a possibility.

ZenotiEnterprise (custom)

RFID, POS, onboarding & analytics
GantnerHardware + SaaS
RFID / lockers

European spa and wellness RFID specialist. Wristband-based locker access, cashless spending throughout facility. Used across major European bathhouses and US spa properties. Get quotes from Gantner and Ojmar — evaluate both after site is confirmed.

Toast$69/mo+
POS

Best if F&B is a major revenue driver. Strong kitchen display and tip flows. If retail is primary, evaluate Book4Time's native POS first — reducing system count reduces integration complexity.

Smartwaiver$39/mo

Digital health intake forms, consent docs, and waiver management. Auto-syncs to guest profiles. Embed in the pre-arrival email flow — don't make waivers a front-desk bottleneck on opening day.

Best-in-class ID + selfie match and age verification. Integrate into the pre-arrival flow, not at the front desk. Pay-per-verification keeps cost proportional to actual usage.

Registrar strategy

Consolidate at Namecheap or Cloudflare Registrar (at-cost, no markup). Point all nameservers to Cloudflare DNS for centralized redirects and SSL.

Email auth order

SPF first, then DKIM — DMARC cannot be configured without at least one in place. All three records live in Cloudflare DNS. Never send outbound email before all three are active.

Core principle: HubSpot is the relationship spine. Every tool that touches a customer syncs back here. Glofox holds billing during Phase 2 — add glofox_member_id to HubSpot contacts now so a future Stripe migration has a clean target.
