The Architect Stack

A modular architecture where HubSpot owns the customer relationship, Stripe owns billing, and Zenoti owns operations — no single vendor controls the whole picture. More configuration upfront than the Experience Stack, but the data architecture survives any platform change.

Stripe is the core complexity driver in Phase 2. Webhook configuration and the HubSpot sync (16 hrs combined) is real developer work — not plug-and-play. The 8 hrs of end-to-end testing is non-negotiable: billing logic failures are invisible until a real customer hits an edge case. The 6-hr buffer specifically accounts for Stripe's documentation density and any client-requested changes to membership tier structure.

The billing advantage: Stripe is the most portable billing layer available. Founding member payment history, subscription logic, and customer IDs live independently of Zenoti or any future ops platform. If any other platform is replaced at Phase 3, billing continuity is fully protected.
